FAQs

What is Social Commerce?

Social commerce is the use of social media platforms to facilitate
online shopping and purchasing of products or services.

How can small businesses benefit from social commerce?

Small businesses can reach a larger audience, engage directly with customers, and implement cost-effective marketing strategies.

Is social commerce suitable for all types of businesses?

Yes, social commerce can be adapted to benefit various types of businesses. Its versatility allows for creative approaches tailored to different industries and business models.

How does social commerce differ from e-commerce?

While e-commerce involves transactions on dedicated online stores or marketplaces, social commerce occurs directly on social media platforms.
